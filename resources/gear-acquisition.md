# Gear Acquisition

**Gear Acquisition** is a spreadsheet listing every weapon, shield, helm, armor, and accessory in the game with every way a player can obtain it.

**Credits:** Eochaid

### What can you do with this?

For all 420 gear items in the game, this sheet tracks rarity and every acquisition path: bazaar recipe, shop, treasure chest, enemy drop, steal/poach, or quest/hunt reward. It is aimed at players and guide writers more than at binary editing, useful if your mod changes drop tables or shop stock and you want to know what a given item's vanilla acquisition looked like first. The sheet is community-editable, though the author asks that edits only fix mistakes rather than restructure it.
