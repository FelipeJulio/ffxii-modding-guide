# Gear Acquisition

**Gear Acquisition** is a spreadsheet listing every weapon, shield, helm, armor, and accessory in the game with every way a player can obtain it.

**Credits:** Eochaid

### What can you do with this?

For all 420 gear items in the game, this sheet tracks rarity and every acquisition path: bazaar recipe, shop, treasure chest, enemy drop, steal/poach, or quest/hunt reward. A single item often has entries in several columns at once (available from both a shop and a chest, for example). It is aimed at players and guide writers more than at binary editing: unlike the Section 13 or Section 57 sheets it sits next to, no editor in The Insurgent's Toolkit actually reads this data, so use it to check what a given item's vanilla acquisition looked like before your mod changes a drop table or shop's stock. The sheet is community-editable, though the author asks that edits only fix mistakes rather than restructure it. For the item's actual stats once you've found it, cross-check Battlepack Section 13: Equipment Stats.
